Version 28 Favorite Journal Auto-Open

- When you tap the star on a scripture, the app immediately opens a focused Favorite Scripture Journal entry.
- The entry is pre-filled with week, topic, reference, and date.
- You can write:
  - Why is this a favorite?
  - What did I learn about Jesus Christ?
  - How can I apply this?
- Save and return to the week.
- Existing progress, favorites, notes, JTC readings, and assessments are preserved.
